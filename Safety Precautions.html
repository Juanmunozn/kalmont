Safety Precautions

Hypothermia safety precautions are essential for both oneself and others, especially when venturing into cold environments. Here are some key precautions:

Safety for Self

  • Dress Appropriately
  • Stay Dry
  • Stay Hydrated and Nourished
  • Take Breaks
  • Be Aware of Early Signs of Hypothermia

Safety for Others

  • Check on Each other
  • Share Resources
  • Provide Assistance
  • Encourage Safe Behaviour
  • Be Prepared to Act
