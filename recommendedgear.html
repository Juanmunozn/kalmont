Recommended Gear

Insulated Clothing

Multiple layers of clothing, including moisture-wicking base layers, insulating mid-layers (such as fleece or wool), and a waterproof outer layer (such as a jacket and pants) to protect against cold, wind, and moisture.

Extra Clothing

Carry spare clothing, including gloves, hats, socks, and additional layers, in case your clothing gets wet or you need extra insulation.

Emergency Shelter

Pack a lightweight emergency shelter, such as a space blanket or bivy sack, to provide protection from the elements and help retain body heat if you need to stop and rest.
